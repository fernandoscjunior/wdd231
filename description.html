Site Name: Digital Oasis

This name is very fitting, since the site is about digital life and ecology, digital is very fitting such as Oasis that relates to nature.

Site Purpose:

The purpose of this site is to provide a ecological digital life by informing and providing tools to help the environment while using every-day technology.

Scenarios:

How can I be more eco-friendly?
Is there any app to help planting trees?
Can I help the planet environment without spending money?
